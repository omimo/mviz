About

The works presented here are the result of having fun with motion capture data, exploring data visualization, and collaborating with fellow researchers during my PhD. Most of the visulizations do not have any scientific purpose and are made just out of curiosity :-)

The visualizations are created using the D3.js library, which is a very powerful tool for creating web-based visualizations out of data. The source-codes for the visualizations are avaibale on github. You are welcome to play with them and create your own visulizations.

If you are interested in a more through tutorial on how to visualize motion capture data using D3.js, you can take a look at this blog post.
